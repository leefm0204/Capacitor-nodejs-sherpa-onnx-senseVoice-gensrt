Configuration Guide

VAD Settings (Voice Activity Detection)

These parameters control how speech segments are detected from audio:

  • Threshold: Speech detection sensitivity
    • Higher (0.6-0.8): Fewer false positives, may miss quiet speech
    • Lower (0.2-0.4): Catches quiet speech, may trigger on noise
    • Recommended: 0.4-0.6 for clean audio, 0.3 for noisy
  • Min Speech: Minimum duration to count as speech
    • Higher (0.5-1.0s): Filters out short utterances
    • Lower (0.1-0.2s): Captures brief words and reactions
    • Recommended: 0.2-0.3s for dialogue
  • Min Silence: Pause length to end a segment
    • Higher (0.8-1.5s): Merges speech into longer segments
    • Lower (0.2-0.4s): Splits at every pause
    • Recommended: 0.4-0.6s for movies
  • Window Size: Analysis window (samples)
    • Larger: Smoother detection, less responsive
    • Smaller: More responsive, may be jittery
    • Recommended: 512 (default)

Recognizer Settings

  • Threads: Processing threads (performance only)
    • Higher (4-8): Faster but more battery usage
    • Lower (1-2): Slower but battery-efficient
  • Language: Target language for transcription
    • Auto Detect: Works for clear, single-language audio
    • Manual: Better for accents or mixed content

Chunk Size Settings

  • Chunk Size: Audio chunk duration sent to transcriber
    • Smaller (0.5-1.0s): More frequent VAD processing, better segment detection, more responsive
    • Larger (3.0-5.0s): Fewer callbacks, slightly more efficient, less granular
    • Recommended: 1.0-2.0s for most content, 0.5-1.0s for rapid dialogue

Tip: Reducing chunk size from 2.0s to 1.0s can improve subtitle segment granularity, especially for fast-paced content.

How to Use Presets

Use the Quick Presets dropdown above to instantly apply optimized settings:

  • 🎬 Movies/TV: Balanced for dialogue over background music
  • 🎤 Lectures: Optimized for clear speech with pauses
  • 🎵 Music/Noisy: Filters out background noise
  • 🗣️ Rapid Dialogue: Catches quick back-and-forth exchanges
  • 📺 News: Professional broadcast pacing
  • 🎙️ Podcasts: Conversational speech patterns
  • 📚 Audiobooks: Continuous narration
  • 💼 Meetings: Multi-speaker discussions

Common Issues

  • Missing quiet dialogue? → Lower Threshold (0.3-0.4)
  • Too many segments? → Increase Min Silence (0.7-1.0s)
  • Subtitles too long? → Decrease Min Silence (0.3-0.4s)
  • Background noise triggers? → Increase Threshold (0.6-0.7)
  • Processing slow? → Increase thread counts
